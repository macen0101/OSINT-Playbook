# OSINT-Playbook

A structured framework for reconnaissance, threat exposure analysis, and social engineering risk assessment. This repository serves as a centralized collection of methodologies, automated workflows, and reporting templates designed to identify organizational security gaps while strictly adhering to ethical disclosure and privacy standards (PIPEDA compliant).

## 🎯Objectives
- Develop repeatable reconnaissance pipelines for cybersecurity assessments
- Identify potential attack vectors through human-centric and technical reconnaissance
- Provide actionable mitigation strategies for identified exposures
